Ingredients

  • 4 lbs duckling
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp finely chopped green onion
  • 1 tsp ginger root
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp superfine sugar
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 2 tsp five-spice powder

Instructions

  1. Clean the duck well and remove lumps of fat
  2. Rub salt over entire duck
  3. Make the marinade
  4. Prepare and blanch the duck
  5. Air-Dry the duck
  6. Roast the duck in 400-degree oven for 25minutes
  7. Serve and enjoy!
