Garlic Oysters

Ingredients

  • 12 Oysters
  • 2 tbsp garlic
  • 4 ounces butter
  • 1 tsp Italian Parsley
  • 1/4 tsp granulated onion
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp Romano cheese

Instructions

  1. Simmer butter and garlic over low heat for 7-10minutes
  2. Remove from heat and add all seasonings except cheese
  3. Place garlic butter into small bowl and refrigerate
  4. Clean oysters under running cold water
  5. Place opened oysters on a cutting board or baking sheet
  6. Spoon a small amount of garlic butter over each oyster
  7. Place Oysters in preheated 400-degree oven for 15-20 minutes
  8. Serve and enjoy!
