Ingredients

  • 4 lobster tails
  • salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup butter melted
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp thyme, rosemary and parsley

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to broil or 500 degrees
  2. Butterfly the tail by cutting down the center
  3. Salt and pepper the meat and set on baking sheet
  4. In a small bowl whisk together the melted butter
  5. Spread on each lobster tail
  6. Broil the lobster tails for about 8-10minutes or until meat is lightly brown
  7. Serve with butter and enjoy!
